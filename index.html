Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Stride Orthotic Lab ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal and clinical information. This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected, used, stored, and protected when using the Stride Orthotic Lab mobile application ("the App").

1. Intended Use

The App is an internal clinical tool intended for use by licensed clinicians, pedorthists, orthotists, and partner clinics for the purpose of capturing foot scan data and submitting custom orthotic orders.

The App is not a consumer-facing application and is not intended for use by patients directly.

2. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following data when used by authorized users:

  • Foot scan data (3D point clouds or mesh files such as PLY)
  • Order-related clinical information entered by the clinician
  • Clinic or practitioner identification details
  • Technical metadata required to operate the App securely

The App does not collect advertising data, behavioral tracking data, or analytics for marketing purposes.

3. How Data Is Used

Collected data is used solely to:

  • Generate and manufacture custom orthotic devices
  • Manage clinical orders and drafts
  • Enable secure storage and retrieval of scan files
  • Maintain the integrity and functionality of the App

Data is not sold, shared for advertising, or used for profiling.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored securely using industry-standard cloud infrastructure. Access is restricted to authorized users and systems involved in order fulfillment.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

5. Authentication

Access to the App requires authenticated credentials. User sessions are managed securely to ensure only authorized clinicians can access stored data.

6. Camera and Sensor Usage

The App uses the device’s front-facing camera and TrueDepth sensor exclusively for capturing 3D foot scans required for clinical orthotic design.

Camera data is used only during active scanning sessions and is not recorded, streamed, or stored outside of the scan files explicitly saved by the clinician.

7. Data Retention

Clinical data and scan files are retained only as long as necessary to fulfill their intended clinical and operational purposes or as required by applicable regulations.

8. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on secure third-party services for authentication, data storage, and infrastructure support. These services are used solely to enable App functionality and are not permitted to use data for their own purposes.

9. Your Responsibilities

Clinics and clinicians using the App are responsible for ensuring that patient data is collected and used in compliance with applicable healthcare and privacy regulations in their jurisdiction.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
